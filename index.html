Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our iOS Clipboard app (the "App").

Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information about you. It only saves text data locally on your iPhone device and syncs it to your iCloud account. We do not have access to the content of your Clipboard or any other personal data stored on your device or iCloud account.

How We Use Your Information

We do not use or access the information stored by the App in any way. The text data saved by the App is solely for your convenience, allowing you to access your copied text across your devices via iCloud synchronization.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your information with third parties. The text data saved by the App is stored securely on your device and iCloud account, and it is not accessible to us or any other external parties.

Your Rights

As the user, you have full control over the text data stored by the App. You can delete or manage this data directly through the App or your iCloud settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes made to the Privacy Policy will be reflected in the updated version of the App available for download.
